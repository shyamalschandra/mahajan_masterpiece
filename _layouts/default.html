Website design inspired by Video Policy project by Carl Vondrick's research group at Columbia University.

The Video Policy project demonstrates how video generation can be used as a proxy for robot policy learning, showing the power of generative models for understanding and predicting behavior. Similarly, this project explores how different deep learning architectures can be applied to ECG classification, with the VAE component demonstrating generative capabilities for explainable AI.

Reference: Liang, J., et al. (2025). "Video Generators are Robot Policies." arXiv preprint arXiv:2508.00795. https://videopolicy.cs.columbia.edu/
